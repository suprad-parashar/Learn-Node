About Us

Learn aims to provide curated educational resources, made available by various educators and content creators around the world, across various domains, to students. We at Learn believe that we must learn the concepts for our better understanding and skill development, not just study them for the sake of marks. Learn provides a platform for Students to access various resources such as Videos, Courses, etc.
